Introduction

Hello, I'm Iestin Evans. I'm currently a Senior at the State University of New York at Fredonia, where I will obtain a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. Since I was a child, I had a love for computers, thus it was a natural choice to decide that I wanted a career path in the computer field. My interests are in Cyber Security, AI, Database Administration, and Game Development. My goal is to get a career in one of those four fields. Some of my interests outside of computers are music, photography and video post-production. I have spent a decent amount of time learning those fields on my own and I've taken a class for each of the those fields. Though while I'm no expert in those fields, I hope to incorporate them more into my life, either with my career or a personal side job.
