Introduction

Hello, I'm Iestin Evans. I'm currently a Senior at the State University of New York at Fredonia, where I will obtain a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. Since I was a child, I had a love for computers, thus it was a natural choice to decide that I wanted a career path in the computer field. My interests are in Cyber Security, AI, Database Administration, and Game Development. My goal is to get a career in one of those four fields. Some of my interests outside of computers are music, photography and video post-production. I have spent a decent amount of time learning those fields on my own and I've taken a class for each of the fields. Though while I'm no expert in these fields, I hope to incorporate these into my life more with my career or a personal side job, as I gain more experience as time goes on.

Game Development

Like many Computer Science students this day and age, I entered this field of study with the dream of getting a career in Game Development. As the semesters went by, I realized that a career goals in Cyber Security, AI, or Database Administration would be better options to me, but I didn't let that stop me from dabbling in Game Development. Over the past couple years, I, along with a colleague, have worked on various small games to learn how to use Unity. Now that we're confident with using Unity, we have started a big project and plan to start a company to put this project under. This has been and will continue to be a great learning experience for us, not only with programming but also with planning, organizing, and dealing with networks.
